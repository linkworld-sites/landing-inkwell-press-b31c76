Why Letterpress

A craft with depth and permanence

In a world of digital noise, letterpress printing creates something tangible, textured, and treasured. Each piece bears the unmistakable mark of the press.

⬛

Deep Impression

Our antique Heidelberg windmill presses create that signature debossed texture — felt with the fingertips, not just seen with the eyes.

🎨

Custom Ink Mixing

From rich earth tones to fluorescent accents, we mix every ink by hand to match your palette with precision and consistency.

📄

Archival Papers

We source acid-free, cotton-rag papers from heritage mills — Crane, Lettra, and Gmund — stocks that age with grace.

✦

Foil & Embossing

Complement your letterpress with blind embossing, hot foil stamping in gold, silver, rose gold, and more.

✉

Bespoke Consultation

Every project begins with a dedicated design consultation. We guide you from concept to finished suite, start to finish.

🌱

Sustainably Sourced

We use soy-based inks, FSC-certified papers, and compostable packaging — craftsmanship that respects the planet.

Our Process

From brief to beautiful

A refined, collaborative workflow designed to produce extraordinary results with minimal stress on your end.

01

Consultation

Share your vision, timeline, and aesthetic preferences. We listen, ask the right questions, and establish your project scope.

02

Design

Our in-house designers craft custom layouts, or we adapt your existing artwork for optimal letterpress reproduction.

03

Proof & Approve

Receive a digital proof and, for larger orders, a physical press test. We refine until every detail is exactly right.

04

Press & Finish

Each sheet is hand-fed through our vintage presses. Finishing touches — trimming, scoring, foiling — are applied by skilled hands.

05

Delivery

Carefully packed and shipped, or collected from our studio. We include extras for keepsakes and assembly mishaps.
